Group No. 7

AcadPath

PURPOSE

This website will assist senior high school students in deciding their future courses by providing ideas aligned with their chosen strand, as well as list of schools that specializes those specific course. It will also offer insights into potential careers associated with their selected courses. Users can also take a quiz that will suggest cources that aligns with their answer. In this website, they can also send a letter to the future self and set the date of when they want to recieve them.

CONTENT

  • Pictures - Pictures that shows what those cources and jobs look like and what they do.
  • Videos - We will get videos from YouTube such as 'What are the things you need to know before taking this course?'
  • Text - The informations will be given through text.
  • Animation - We will use them for design.
  • Graphics - We will include some graphics to make it more attractive for the users.
